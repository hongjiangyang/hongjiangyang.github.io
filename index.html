About

Seeing miracles in the ordinary.

Hongjiang Yang is an Associate Investigator at the Shenzhen Medical Academy of Research and Translation. His research focuses on using high-throughput in vivo recording technologies to understand the neural mechanisms underlying complex behavior.

He received his Ph.D. in Physics from the University of Science and Technology of China in 2018. From 2019 to 2021, he conducted postdoctoral research in neuroscience at Tsinghua University. From 2021 to 2024, he was an Assistant Researcher at the Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology and a visiting scholar at the Southern University of Science and Technology.

阳弘江，现任深圳医学科学院副研究员，致力于利用高通量在体记录技术解析复杂行为的神经机制。 研究结合先进的成像、数据分析与计算建模方法，揭示神经活动与行为之间的多尺度联系。
